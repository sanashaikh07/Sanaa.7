About Doraemon

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century, created by Japanese artist Fujiko F. Fujio. He travels back in time to help a boy named Nobita. Doraemon has a magical 4-dimensional pocket filled with futuristic gadgets to solve problems. His birthday is on September 3, 2112, and he is one of the most loved cartoon characters in the world.
