🤖 Who is Doraemon?


Doraemon is a blue robotic cat 🐱 from the 22nd century 🕒. He was sent to the past by a boy named Sewashi to help his ancestor, Nobita Nobi. Doraemon has a magical pocket 🎒 full of futuristic gadgets that solve everyday problems in fun ways. He is kind, helpful, and super cute!

📋 Doraemon's Quick Info

  • Name: Doraemon
  • Type: Robot Cat 🤖🐱
  • Color: Blue 💙
  • Birthday: September 3, 2112 🎂
  • Favorite Food: Dorayaki 🍡
  • Fear: Mice 🐭 (because they bit off his ears!)
  • Height & Weight: 129.3 cm & 129.3 kg 📏⚖️

🧠 Why Doraemon Came to the Past?

Nobita’s life was full of problems 😓. His future was sad. So, Sewashi sent Doraemon back in time ⏳ to improve Nobita's life and help him make better choices. Now Doraemon lives with Nobita and helps him every day with cool gadgets 🛠️.

🛠️ Top 5 Gadgets from Doraemon’s Pocket

  1. 🚪 Anywhere Door – Travel anywhere instantly!
  2. 🚁 Take-copter – A propeller that helps you fly.
  3. ⏰ Time Machine – Travel through time via Nobita’s desk!
  4. 🎭 Dress-up Camera – Instantly change outfits!
  5. 🧽 Memorystick – Help Nobita study fast!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



Q1: Was Doraemon always blue?

🅰️ No! He was yellow originally. After mice bit his ears 🐭, he cried a lot and turned blue due to sadness 😭.

Q2: What is Doraemon scared of?

🅰️ Mice! He totally panics when he sees one 😱.

Q3: Who created Doraemon?

🅰️ Fujiko F. Fujio ✍️, the famous Japanese manga artist duo.

Q4: Who will marry Nobita?

🅰️ Shizuka Minamoto 💕 – Nobita’s childhood crush and future wife!

Q5: What is Doraemon’s sister’s name?

🅰️ Dorami! She is yellow and very smart 🧠.
